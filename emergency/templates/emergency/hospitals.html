Essential Travel Health Tips

Altitude Awareness

Ascend gradually above 2,500m. Watch for headaches, nausea, and fatigue. Descend if symptoms worsen.

Stay Hydrated

Drink 3-4 liters daily at altitude. Avoid alcohol and excessive caffeine. Monitor urine color.

Food Safety

Eat hot, freshly cooked food. Avoid raw vegetables and tap water in remote areas.

Emergency Kit

Carry pain relievers, anti-diarrheal medication, bandages, and personal prescriptions.
