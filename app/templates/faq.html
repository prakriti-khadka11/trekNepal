Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about TrekNepal

Booking & Payment

Browse our packages, select your preferred trek/tour, choose your travel date and number of people, then proceed to payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once payment is successful.

Yes! Go to "My Bookings" and click the "Cancel" button. Cancellation policies apply based on how far in advance you cancel.

We accept payments through Khalti (e-wallet, banking, cards). All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Guides & Communication

Once your booking is confirmed and a guide is assigned, you'll see a "Message Guide" button in your bookings. Click it to start chatting directly with your guide.

Yes! All guides must submit their license certificates and are verified by our admin team before being assigned to bookings.

Emergency & Safety

Click the red emergency button (bottom right) to access SOS alerts, emergency contacts, nearby hospitals, and altitude sickness tracking. In critical situations, call 112 (universal emergency number).

Our Altitude Safety feature helps you track symptoms, create acclimatization plans, and get recommendations. Access it from the main menu under "Altitude Safety".

Account & Profile
