Using a Pulse Oximeter
  • Place the sensor on your fingertip, ensuring good contact
  • Keep your hand still and at heart level during measurement
  • Wait for the reading to stabilize (usually 10-30 seconds)
  • Take readings at rest, not immediately after physical activity
  • Cold fingers can affect accuracy - warm them up first
Oxygen Monitoring Guidelines

Check oxygen levels twice daily: morning and evening

Track trends over time - sudden drops are more concerning than gradual changes

If SpO2 drops below 85% or you feel unwell, descend immediately

Combine with symptom monitoring for complete altitude health assessment

Oxygen levels naturally drop during sleep - morning readings may be lower
