Acclimatization Guidelines

Above 3000m, don't increase sleeping altitude by more than 300-500m per day

Take a rest day every 3-4 days above 3000m

Follow "climb high, sleep low" principle when possible

Stay well hydrated and avoid alcohol

Monitor symptoms daily and descend if severe symptoms occur

Safety Reminders
  • Never ascend with severe altitude sickness symptoms
  • Descend immediately if symptoms worsen despite rest
  • Carry emergency communication devices
  • Know evacuation routes and emergency contacts
  • Consider travel insurance that covers high-altitude rescue
